About Me

Hello, I'm Babysauro. By day, I'm a diligent engineering student. But when the sun sets and free time emerges, I transform into a passionate musician.
Music is my hidden passion, a way to express myself in ways that formulas and calculations can't capture. I'm a multi-instrumentalist with an insatiable thirst for knowledge and a burning desire to explore new sounds.
Through music, I aim to convey emotions and experiences that surround me, turning each note into an opportunity to tell a story. Among my compositions, you'll find mainly instrumental pieces, where notes become my brushes and silence my canvas.
Some of my creations have even been chosen to enrich the gaming experience of "Shattered", an adventure that I hope can transport you to new and fascinating worlds. If you'd like to delve deeper into my musical world, I invite you to visit the "Shattered" website, where you can listen to my creations and perhaps discover something new along the way.
Thank you for being here, and I hope my music can add a splash of color to your life.
